How to Play

  • You have 6 guesses in order to guess the word
  • Type in your guess in the form of a letter A-Z into the input box.
  • Once you want to guess, click "Check Guess".
  • If you are right, the letter you chose will be revealed.
  • Once you guess all the letters, you win.
  • If you are wrong, you will lose a guess, and the hangman will gain a limb.
  • Once the hangman is completed at 0 guesses left, you lose.
